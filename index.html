About

A love for Acting, Movement & Art.

Tahzara Sophia Wurawa is an Austrian actress. She received her acting Diploma at Pinewood Studios in England as well as the Vienna English Theatre Academy in Austria.

She has a love for Acting, dancing, kick boxing, movement and singing. Tahzara also loves working with her hands such as creating miniature sets, drawing, painting and sculpturing.

About

A dedication to truth in every character.

Elena Voss is an award-nominated actress known for her transformative performances across stage and screen. Trained at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art, her work has been described as raw, compelling, and effortlessly authentic.

From independent cinema to West End productions, Elena brings an unwavering commitment to her craft — finding the humanity in every role, no matter how complex. Her recent work includes lead roles in acclaimed productions at the National Theatre and a critically praised performance in the feature film "Between Silences."
